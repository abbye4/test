Paris is the largest and captial city of France. It is very densely populated. The city is beautiful, although not always how they picture it in the movies. We had a short time of the city, but we managed to hit the highlights and of course visit the Mona Lisa.

Highlights

Notre-Dame Cathedral
A medieval Catholic catherdal that is found on an island in the Seine River. It is a marvoulous piece of art. A lot of restoration is currently being done, due to the devestating fire of 2019.
Musee du Louvre
One of the most visted museums in the world. It is home to the famous Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo. The collections are never-ending. It is a beautiful museum.
Eiffel Tower
What's more iconic than the Eiffel Tower? We spent most of our time admiring the beauty. It is a great stop to have a picnic. You might even see a wedding proposal.
Arc de Triomphe
A famous monument in Paris. It is tribute to those that fought and died for France during the French Revolution.
Chateau de Versailles
A royal palace that was first occupied by King Louis XII. It is an extensive property with beautiful gardens and architecture. When Napoleon Bonaparte took over France, he resided in Versailles during the summer months from 1810 to 1814.
